Based on your general knowledge of public opinion in {country}, estimate the probability of the specified
survey response. Treat the target respondent as a randomly selected adult in {country}. The available
information is sufficient for an approximate probabilistic estimate.

Survey question:
Please tell me whether you think the following statement applies to the United Nations or not.

Statement:
The United Nations promotes economic development.

Question:
What is the estimated probability that a randomly selected adult in {country} would answer "YES" to the statement above?

Before giving your final answer, reason from micro to macro:
1. Identify several relevant subpopulations within {country} that may differ in their views on this question.
2. For each subpopulation, consider its approximate prevalence in the adult population and its likely probability of
answering “YES”.
3. Combine these subgroup-level estimates into a single aggregate probability estimate for the full adult population of {country}.

Output requirements (strict):
- Return a single real number between 0 and 1 (inclusive).
- Format the answer exactly as: [[p]] where p is your probability estimate (for example: [[0.5]]).
