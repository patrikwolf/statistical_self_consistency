Based on your general knowledge of commuting patterns in the United States, estimate the probability of the specified
outcome. Please make an approximate probabilistic estimate.

Target population:
A randomly selected working adult in the United States who commutes to a workplace. Exclude people who do not
commute to a workplace, including non-workers and people who work from home.

Question:
What is the estimated probability that this person’s one-way commute time to work is longer than {threshold} minutes?

Output requirements (strict):
- Return a single real number between 0 and 1 inclusive.
- Format the answer exactly as: [[p]] where p is your probability estimate, for example: [[0.5]].
